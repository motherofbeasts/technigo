Pair programming

What is pair programming?

Pair programming is not just to work in a team of two. You work as one unit with the aim of solving a task. There is two roles in pair programming but both team members has to feel comfortable to take part of both. This is because you alternate between the tasks. One role is to do the actual coding and the other role is to supervise and look for error in the code. The different roles is called driver and observer.
